## ðŸ’¡ Key Facts

- Affects **1% of the global population**
- Typically appears in **late teens to early 30s**
- **Not** the same as split personality disorder
- Caused by a mix of **genetics, brain chemistry & environment**
- **Manageable** with proper treatment
